# IMIC – Clarification Questions (Functional Requirements)

The following clarification questions are intended to remove ambiguity from the **functional requirements** of the IMIC system and support accurate design, development, and testing.

| Requirement ID / Use Case ID | Requirement Area | Question | Response (Example) | Stakeholder | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| FR-01 / UC-05 | Policy Purchase | Can a customer buy more than one active policy at the same time? | No, only one active policy per customer is allowed. | Insurance Company | Open |
| FR-02 / UC-05 | Policy Purchase | Is online/direct purchase without an agent allowed in future? | Not in Phase 1; agent-only purchase. | Business | Open |
| FR-03 / UC-05 | Policy Members | Is there a maximum number of dependents allowed per policy? | Maximum 5 dependents per policy. | Insurance Company | Open |
| FR-04 / UC-05 | Policy Members | Are dependents restricted by relationship type (spouse, child, parent)? | Allowed relationships: spouse, children, parents only. | Business | Open |
| FR-05 / UC-05 | Documentation | What document formats are supported for uploads? | PDF and JPG only. | IT / Architecture | Open |
| FR-06 / UC-05 | Documentation | Is document upload mandatory at submission or can it be partial? | All required documents must be uploaded before submission. | Insurance Company | Open |
| FR-07 / UC-07 | Documentation | Are medical reports validated manually or automatically? | Manual validation by company staff. | Operations | Open |
| FR-08 / UC-05 | Policy Cover | What is the minimum and maximum policy cover amount? | Min ₹5,00,000; Max ₹25,00,000. | Business | Open |
| FR-09 / UC-05 | Premium Calculation | Are premium calculation rules configurable? | Yes, via admin configuration. | IT / Business | Open |
| FR-10 / UC-05 | Premium Calculation | Is premium recalculated if member details are updated before approval? | Yes, premium is recalculated automatically. | Business | Open |
| FR-11 / UC-06 | Payment | When is payment collected – before or after policy approval? | Before approval at submission time. | Finance | Open |
| FR-12 / UC-06 | Payment | What payment modes are supported? | Cheque only (Phase 1). | Finance | Open |
| FR-13 / UC-07 | Policy Approval | Is partial approval allowed for some members? | No, rejection of any member rejects entire policy. | Insurance Company | Confirmed |
| FR-14 / UC-07 | Policy Approval | Are rejection reasons standardized or free text? | Select from predefined list with optional remarks. | Operations | Open |
| FR-15 / UC-07 | Policy Issuance | How are policy documents delivered to policy holder? | Email and physical courier. | Operations | Open |
| FR-16 / UC-07 | Credentials | Are usernames auto-generated or user-defined? | Auto-generated by system. | IT | Open |
| FR-17 / UC-09 | Policy Holder Portal | Can policy holders update personal details themselves? | No, view-only access. | Business | Confirmed |
| FR-18 / UC-11 | Claims | Is there a limit on number of claims per policy/year? | Maximum 3 claims per policy year. | Insurance Company | Open |
| FR-19 / UC-11 | Claims | Can claims be raised for dependents independently? | Yes, but routed through the same policy. | Business | Open |
| FR-20 / UC-11 | Claims | Are claims allowed only at network hospitals? | Network hospitals only in Phase 1. | Insurance Company | Open |
| FR-21 / UC-11 | Claims Documents | Are original documents required or scanned copies sufficient? | Scanned copies sufficient for submission. | Operations | Open |
| FR-22 / UC-12 | Claim Processing | Is claim approval SLA defined? | 7 working days from submission. | Operations | Open |
| FR-23 / UC-12 | Claim Payment | How is payment made for accepted claims? | Cheque issued to policy holder. | Finance | Open |
| FR-24 / UC-08 | Agent Portal | Can an agent view policies created by other agents? | No, agents can view only their own policies. | Business | Open |
| FR-25 / UC-05 | Agent Portal | Can agents edit policy details after submission? | No, editing is disabled after submission. | Business | Open |
| FR-26 / UC-07 | Company Portal | Can company staff override rejection rules? | No overrides allowed. | Compliance | Open |
| FR-27 / UC-01 | Security | Should users be notified when their account is locked? | Yes, via on-screen message only. | Security | Open |
| FR-28 / UC-07, UC-12 | Audit | Should policy and claim actions be audit-logged? | Yes, all approve/reject actions. | Compliance | Open |
| FR-29 / UC-07, UC-12 | Notifications | Are email/SMS notifications required for status changes? | Email only in Phase 1. | Business | Open |

---

**Status Legend**
- **Open** – Clarification required from stakeholder
- **Confirmed** – Requirement already agreed

> This table can be directly used for BRD/FRD sign-off, sprint grooming, and traceability reviews.
